Bio

My name is Logan Douglass. I do a lot of things, but currently I am beginning my career as a web developer, data scientist, and software engineer. Beyond music, some of my other passions include martial arts and video gaming. I have been gaming since I was 3-years-old, and have a reputation for being something of a Smash wiz. Contact me if you're interested in content creation or preparation for professional play.
